Reserve a Telescope

Complete the form below to reserve telescope time.



Telescope time is given out by day, so you will have all day with our 5-meter reflector. You will also have access to our spectroscopy equipment and solar observation filter. We do ask that you give us at least 48 hours notice for solar observation. This will give our techs time to do the filter installation and conversion.

Please contact Marcus Brody to complete pre and post telescope usage reports.

Available Slots for the 5-meter Telescope


Select "show schedule" to show time slots for each week, and choose your slot by selecting the radio button and clicking "Submit." You will get a confirmation popup on successful reservation.
